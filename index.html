MANIFEST

The clock is ticking. Time is running out.

Our leaders cling to power. Wars are fought for profit and pride. Children die for borders and flags.
Our planet is burning.
The system keeps us trapped — working to survive, not to live.

Praying alone is not enough.
Complaining alone is not enough.
Doing nothing ends in darkness.

To protect the light, we must act.
Now. Together.

Technology can secure food, shelter, and care for all.
But without ethics, progress becomes extinction.

We are one humanity. We are one family. With one home.

ONE WORLD. ONE NATION.
ONE LOVE. ONE FUTURE.

TIME IS RUNNING OUT. NOW IS THE MOMENT.
